# 📚 Book Tracking App

## 📖 Overview

This application is designed to take reading as a hobby to the next level. Registered users can track their reading habits, organize their personal libraries, and gain insights into their reading activity through detailed statistics and achievements.

The goal of the app is to create a personalized digital reading experience where users can document, evaluate, and celebrate their reading journey.

---

# ✨ Features

## 👤 User Registration & Authentication

* Secure user registration and login
* Personalized user profiles
* User-specific reading data and statistics

---

## 📚 Virtual Library

Users can create and manage their own virtual library.

Each book can include:

* Title
* Author
* ISBN
* Publication year
* Cover image upload
* Additional custom information

---

## ⭐ Ratings & Reviews

Users can evaluate books by:

* Giving a star rating from 1 to 5
* Writing detailed text reviews

---

## 📝 Wishlist

* Save books for future reading
* Organize planned books separately from completed reads

---

## 📊 Reading Statistics

The application generates detailed annual statistics, including:

* Number of books read
* Total pages read
* Reading activity throughout the year

Users can also indicate whether they read on specific days of a month. This information is used to generate more accurate annual statistics and reading habit analytics.

---

## 🏆 Achievement System

Users can unlock achievements and awards by completing specific goals, such as:

* Reading streaks
* Number of completed books
* Pages read milestones
* Consistent monthly activity

---

# 🎯 Project Goal

The purpose of this project is to motivate readers, help them organize their reading habits, and provide a fun and rewarding experience through statistics and achievements.
